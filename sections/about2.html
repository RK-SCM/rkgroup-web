Our Story

Rajakumari Group embarked on its journey in 1996, starting as a jewelry shop named 'Rajadhani' in Kallambalam. With a bold vision for growth, it rebranded as Rajakumari Group, evolving into a trusted name renowned for blending tradition with innovation. Today, the Group stands as a symbol of excellence, redefining quality and transforming customer experiences across diverse industries.

From luxurious jewelry showrooms to vibrant wedding centers, innovative hypermarkets, restaurants, and malls, Rajakumari Group brings a unique blend of tradition and innovation to cater to modern lifestyles.

Our operations encompass a wide array of ventures, including shopping malls, gold & diamond jewelry showrooms, hypermarkets, and wedding centers.

Powered by a dedicated team of over 1,700 professionals, our operations deliver exceptional value and innovation, setting new benchmarks in every industry we touch.

Beyond business, our commitment to social impact is at the heart of what we do. Through our charitable trust , we actively contribute to society with initiatives like free dialysis centers and programs that enrich lives and bring about positive change.

With a legacy spanning nearly three decades, Rajakumari Group continues to lead with integrity, innovation, and a customer-first approach, creating experiences that inspire trust and loyalty.
