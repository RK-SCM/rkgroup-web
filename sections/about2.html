Our Story

Rajakumari Group stands as a beacon of excellence, leading diverse industries with innovation and integrity. From luxurious jewelry showrooms to innovative hypermarkets, wedding centers, restaurants, and malls, we are committed to redefining quality and elevating the customer experience.

With a strong presence across Kerala and Tamil Nadu, our operations are powered by a dedicated team of over 1,700 professionals. Their unwavering focus on delivering value and innovation drives our continued growth and sets new benchmarks in the industry.

Beyond business, our commitment to social impact is at the heart of what we do. Through our charitable trust, we champion community welfare with initiatives like free dialysis centers and programs that enrich lives and foster positive change.
