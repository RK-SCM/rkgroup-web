About Us

Rajakumari Bullion is a well-established name in the precious metals and jewelry market, known for its reliability and quality. Based in Trissur and Trivandrum, it plays a significant role in Kerala's bullion and jewelry industry, serving as a trusted partner for traders, jewelers, and customers seeking gold and silver bullion.

With decades of experience, Rajakumari Bullion has become synonymous with trust, offering certified products that meet the highest standards of purity and transparency, ensuring customer satisfaction at every step.
