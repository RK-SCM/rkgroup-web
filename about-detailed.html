Our Story

Rajakumari Group has established itself as a leader in diverse industries. From premium jewelry showrooms to innovative hypermarkets, wedding centers, restaurants, and malls, we redefine quality and service.

Located across Kerala and Tamil Nadu, our vast network is powered by a dedicated team of over 50,000 employees, ensuring customer satisfaction and innovation at every step.

Through our charitable trust, we actively engage in community welfare, providing free dialysis centers and other impactful initiatives that make a difference in people's lives.
